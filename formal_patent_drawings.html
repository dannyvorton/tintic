Formal Patent Drawings

The patent drawing is an important component of the patent application and must show every feature of the product. We prepare formal design and utility patent drawings for patent applications being filed with The United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO). We will produce the required drawings of your product that comply with the guidelines set forth by the USPTO.

Pricing

The price for patent drawings depends on the complexity of the product and the illustrations needed. Prices are calculated on a per-figure and per-sheet basis. Contact us for a customized quote.
