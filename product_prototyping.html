Product Prototyping

Prototyping is the process of transitioning a product from the virtual world into the physical world. Through prototyping the design functionality is verified, materials are tested, manufacturing and packing processes are developed, and human factors and ergonomics are improved. Prototyping also facilitates the product communication to the stakeholders and can help reduce costs and minimize risk. Prototypes require multiple iterations and there is a lot that can be learned from each one.
