We helped our client D2 Sports design and prototype their innovative product they call the Coaches Caddy. The Coaches Caddy was designed to carry baseball and softball equipment to and from the sports complex. The D2 Sports Athletic Bag was designed with multiple pockets to carry up to four bats and a variety of baseball and softball gear. The Coaches Caddy also folds up for easy travel and storage.
