RADCAP

Kevin Bullard has over thirty years of experience working as a mechanic yet couldn't find a proper tool to remove radiator caps, so he invented one. Tintic helped him design and prototype his invention, the RADCAP radiator cap wrench.

D2 Sports

Tintic collaborated with D2 Sports to design and prototype an innovative product and thus the Coaches Caddy was born. The Coaches Caddy was designed to carry baseball and softball equipment to and from the sports complex. The Coaches Caddy will carry two baseball and softball buckets, hitting equipment, propane tanks, water coolers, along with the D2 Sports Athletic Bag that attaches to the Coaches Caddy Handle. The D2 Sports Athletic Bag was designed with multiple pockets to carry up to four bats and a variety of baseball and softball gear. The Coaches Caddy also folds up for easy travel and storage.
